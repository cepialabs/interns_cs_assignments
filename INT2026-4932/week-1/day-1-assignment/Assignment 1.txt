			What Cyber security Professionals Actually Do

Summery:

-So mainly Cyber security is the practice of protecting computers , networks , data from unauthorized access or damage.

-It protect personal data , bank credentials from hackers and cyber criminals.

-There are 3 types of ethical hackers.
 1.Red Team (Attacker Team)
 2.Blue Team (Defender Team))
 3.Purple team

1.Red Team 
-Simulate real cyber attack
-Find Vulnerabilities
-Main objective break into system legally to identify weakness

Methods used-
-Reconnaissance (info gathering)
-Exploitation (Finding Weaknessesin system)
-Reporting weakness

Tools-Metasploit , Nmap , Burp suite , Kali Linux

2.Blue Team
-Protect systems 24/7.
-Monitor logs and traffic.
-Detects threats 

Methods used
-Continuous Monitoring(Watching Network traffic)
-Threat detection

Tools-SIEM , Firewall , Soc 

3.Purple Team
-It is combination of Blue and Red Team
-They both work together
-Share attack and defence
