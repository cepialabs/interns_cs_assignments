# Attack Surface Map

## Target
- Website: singhgad.edu
- Organization: Sinhgad Institutes
- Purpose: Educational reconnaissance only


## 1. Exposed Websites and Portals

The following subdomains and portals are publicly accessible:

- admissions.singhgad.edu (Admission portal)
- dashboard.admissions.singhgad.edu (Admin dashboard)
- alumni.singhgad.edu (Alumni portal)
- cms.singhgad.edu (Content management system)
- app.singhgad.edu (Application services)
- cpanel.app.singhgad.edu (Admin control panel)
- mail.app.singhgad.edu (Email service)
- simca.singhgad.edu (Academic portal)
- essportal.singhgad.edu (Employee portal)

These portals may contain **login pages and sensitive data**, which increases security risk.
